Detailed Description

CubeData: one cell of the 32×32×32 spatial partition grid

x/y/z are 5-bit grid coords (0-31). unk0_4 splits the NodeProps: [0..unk0_4) are regular spawns, [unk0_4..prop1Cnt) are events.
